In order for you to experience lasting change, it is highly recommended that we visit once per week for at least 5 sessions, and then every 2 weeks beyond. Cooking Instruction

I teach beginners and those who are already fairly comfortable in the kitchen how to prepare delicious meals that support your overall well-being. I love to address specific health concerns and dietary needs and preferences, including therapeutic diets! Cooking classes are also great for partners, families, and friends to get together and have a good time.

Prices may vary based on your preferences and other factors, such as subject matter and setting. If you are interested in a series of cooking classes, or learning in conjunction with nutrition counseling and/or personal chef service, prices will be discounted.

Cooking classes take place at Nourish Northwest, and last about 2 hours.
